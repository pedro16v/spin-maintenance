Immediate Maintenance

Day 1 ‚Äî Do First

  • Clean & remove rust using WD-40 or Inox
  • Lubricate chain (or check belt tension)
  • Inspect brake pad, sand lightly if glazed
  • Tighten all bolts: pedals, cranks, seat, bars
  • Grease seat & handlebar posts
  • Check pedals & crank bearings
  • Ensure levelers & wheels work smoothly

Bi-Weekly Check

Every 2 Weeks

  • Wipe bike after workouts
  • Check seat & handlebar tightness
  • Wipe flywheel & inspect brake pad

Monthly Service

Every Month

  • Re-lubricate chain
  • Clean & grease adjustment posts
  • Tighten crank & pedal bolts
  • Check belt tension (if applicable)
  • Remove minor rust immediately
